# AI In High School Education

Research and planning materials for a June 2026 discussion with local high school STEM teachers about AI in education and the workplace.

## Focus

- Current state of GenAI use in high school
- Workplace AI skills and candidate expectations
- Practical classroom guidance for STEM teachers
- Assignment redesign and evidence of student thinking
- AI ethics, detection, privacy, and youth-safety concerns
- AI support for student projects, capstones, robotics, and camps
